ABOUT US

VISION:

to build a healthy community where everyone is taken cared of, physically, socially, emotionally and economically.

MISSION STATEMENT:

Providing and promoting comprehensive health care services to people infected by chronic diseases and disabilities as well as giving support and counseling to the affected families. to build a healthy community where everyone is taken cared of, physically, socially, emotionally and economically.

HISTORY:

Soweto Home based care was established in 2002 in response to the high demand for care workers who were assigned to take care of bedridden patients within our community. Our care workers are qualified and acquired the much needed skills to perform their tasks.

OBJECTIVES:

to render home based care services to the community of soweto monday to friday. to transfer skills on home based care services to the youth and family memebers of those that we assist. to give the children a platform that is safe to learn and also to share and recieve assistance. work hand in hand with all networks and organization that share a similar vision as our selves in commmunity development.

Programmes

Home Based Care

Providing and promoting comprehensive health care services to people infected by chronic diseases and disabilities as well as giving support and counseling to the affected families.

After School Mural Support

Each day our centre is flooded with young girls and boys who come through to get assisted with their home work and other school activities. we also have our own extra curriculum that gives art classes.

Feeding Scheme

250 children twice a week recieve a hot meal that sustains them.The feeding scheme is one of our pride and joy because we know that we have reached the most deserving and in need beneficiaries.

Gardening

with little funding we didnt want to have an excuse to not feed our beloved beneficiaries. so we then established a food garden that makes produce that assist us in our soup kitchen and also make an income for administrative purposes.

Granny's Club

The aged members of our community have a programme within our center where they part take in activities that are aimed at psycho-social, emotion physical well-being.Enjoying themselves in the process.
